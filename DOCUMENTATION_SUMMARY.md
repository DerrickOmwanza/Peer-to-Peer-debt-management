## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (BRIEF ANSWERS)

**Q: Is this a bank?**  
A: No. We don't hold money. M-Pesa does.

**Q: Will CBK shut us down?**  
A: Unlikely if we follow rules. Key risk: licensing requirement.

**Q: Why is this better than M-Pesa Fuliza?**  
A: Fuliza is instant credit. We're formal P2P lending with community trust.

**Q: How is this different from SACCOs?**  
A: SACCOs are offline, slow, limited to members. We're digital, fast, open.

**Q: Can this scale to millions of users?**  
A: Yes, if execution is excellent and partnerships hold.

**Q: What's the biggest risk?**  
A: Regulatory uncertainty (CBK licensing unclear).

**Q: What's the biggest opportunity?**  
A: Market is KES 500B+, zero direct competition, strong demand.
